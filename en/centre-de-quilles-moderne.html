Assomption

3115 Boulevard de l'Assomption



Image Credit: Quilles JGNC (quillesjgnc.com)



This 42-lane bowling alley is located right next to Assomption station in HoMa. All lanes are 100% duckpin lanes (small balls). A snack stand and video lottery area are also available. Loto-Québec lottery tickets can even be purchased here.
