EXO COMMUTER TRAIN LINE STATIONS      Back to home page

EXO (formerly Agence Métropolitaine de Transport) is a commuter train line service that connects the Island of Montreal to outer communities on the North and South Shores. EXO also operates bus services that are locally managed by the RTL, the STL and other transit corporations in outer communities at Radisson, Angrignon, Papineau, Montmorency, Cartier, Henri-Bourassa and Bonaventure stations. Its central hub is at Gare Centrale, located at Bonaventure station. The stations that are served by EXO's commuter train line service are listed below.

  • De La Concorde
  • Sauvé
  • Bonaventure
  • Lucien-L'Allier
  • Vendôme
  • Parc
