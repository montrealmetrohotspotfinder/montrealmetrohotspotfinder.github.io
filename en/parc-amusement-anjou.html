Radisson + Bus 44

7500 Boulevard Henri-Bourassa Est



Image Credit: MontrealMiniGolf.ca (montrealminigolf.ca)



In the Anjou borough within walking distance of R.D.P., on Boulevard Henri-Bourassa, lies Parc Amusement Anjou, which has two 18-hole miniature golf courses and a go-kart track. Tournaments are held on the go-kart track annually. It typically opens around mid-May and closes in late September, weather permitting. There is also a snack bar on site.
