Bonaventure or McGill

1 Place Ville Marie



Image Credit: Victor Ruggi (photo taken by me in 2017) (Contact)



Place Ville Marie is a mall and office complex in downtown Montreal. Inaugurated in 1962, it is home to numerous stores and the Le Cathcart food hall among others. It previously housed a 360-degree observatory on its top floor prior to the COVID-19 pandemic; the Les Enfants Terribles restaurant on the floor right below what was once the observatory remains. The complex consists of five office towers and the main tower is 1 Place Ville Marie.


It is connected to Bonaventure (via the Fairmont Reine-Elizabeth and Gare Centrale) & McGill (via the Centre Eaton) stations via the Underground City.
