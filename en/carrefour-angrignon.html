Angrignon

7077 Boulevard Newman



Image Credit: Westcliff Group (westcliff.ca)



Carrefour Angrignon is the flagship mall of the LaSalle borough of Montreal. Built in 1986, it is located along Boulevard Newman and is only footsteps away from Angrignon station and Parc Angrignon. It has a food court, a Maxi supermarket, several other stores, a La Cage sports bar and one of the last remaining Famous Players movie theatres in Canada.


Across from Carrefour Angrignon is a Walmart Supercentre with a McDonald's.
