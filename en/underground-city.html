UNDERGROUND CITY STATIONS      Back to home page

The Underground City (known in French as le Montréal souterrain) refers to a series of interconnected hotels, malls, office towers and other notable buildings that can be accessed entirely without having to step onto the street. Its main stretch covers three Green Line stations and four Orange Line stations in downtown Montreal.
