Last Train

Since all metro stations and their platforms open at different times, some a few minutes before and some a few minutes after 6am, 7am is used as the start time here since all stations are open at that time every day of the week, so all clocks will count down from 7am until closing time. All clocks are in the Eastern Time Zone.
