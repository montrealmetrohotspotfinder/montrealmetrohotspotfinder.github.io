PARC DAN-HANGANU      Back to home page

De L'Église + Bus 12 or Square-Victoria–OACI + Bus 168

260 Rue Elgar



Image Credit: Anik Mercure (amercure.ca)



Parc Dan-Hanganu (formerly Parc Elgar) is a park and green space in the Île des Soeurs area of Verdun. It has a play area, picnic tables and tennis courts. It also contains a miniature strip mall in an area called "Place de l'Unité", which includes a Couche-Tard convenience store, an Italian restaurant, and a Christian, Jewish and Muslim community centre.
