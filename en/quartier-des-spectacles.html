QUARTIER DES SPECTACLES STATIONS      Back to home page

The Quartier des Spectacles is the theatre district of Montreal. It is the site of the annual Montreal International Jazz Festival, the Just For Laughs Festival, the Festival Montréal en Lumière, and many others. Its biggest attraction is the Place des Arts performing arts theatre.
