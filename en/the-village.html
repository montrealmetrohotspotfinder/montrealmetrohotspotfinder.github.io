THE VILLAGE STATIONS      Back to home page

The Village (known in French as Le Village) is the district of Montreal that is home to the city's LGBTQ community. Most of the major attractions of The Village can be found on Rue Sainte-Catherine. The Village plays host to the city's annual Pride parade every August.
